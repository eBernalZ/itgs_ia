Martin Jimenez Moreno

Email: a00904856@itesm.mx

Martin is currently an engineering PhD candidate at Monterrey’s Institute of Technology and Higher Education. He obtained his master’s degree in material’s science at the research center for advanced materials, Monterrey unit (CIMAV), where he studied the interfaces of transition metal nitrides for chalcogenide based semiconductors. His doctoral research investigates the full integration of electroanalytical techniques into centrifugal microfluidic platforms. His interdisciplinary research interests include the development of sample to answer systems for healthcare diagnostics and water quality assessment, the use of centrifugal microfluidics as micro total analysis systems (μTAS) and the development of biosensors and biomaterials based on functional hydrogel-based coatings and carbon MEMS technologies.
