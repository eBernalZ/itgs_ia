About Tec de Monterrey


Tecnologico de Monterrey is a leading institution worldwide (currently holding the 178th position) and in Latin America (currently holding the 6th position). This renowned institution brings together experts from all over the world and trains students with entrepreneur spirit regardless of the discipline.

Image taken from Tec de Monterrey's Instagram


The nanotechnology program is intensely cooperated and interconnected with world-class institutions such as Massachusetts Institute of Technology, Harvard, Stanford University, University of California, Berkeley, and University California Irvine among others. The Ph.D. projects are supervised by prominent scientists of Tecnologico de Monterrey while the research group is highly benefited from the presence of star professors from the abovementioned world-class institutions.
